Use campaigns when you want to send a one-time message to a selected audience.

Campaigns are useful for announcements, promotions, product launches, restocks, event reminders, and other moments where the same message should reach many customers at once.

## Campaigns vs. automations

Use a **campaign** when you decide the audience, message, and send time.

Use a **playbook or automation** when Hellotext should react automatically to customer behavior, such as a subscription, cart event, purchase, or support conversation.

## Before you create a campaign

Make sure you know:

- Who should receive the message.
- Which channel you will use.
- Whether the audience has consent for that channel.
- What action customers should take after reading.
- Which links, coupons, or products need to be included.
- Whether the send should happen now or later.

## Send to the right audience

Most campaigns perform better when they are focused.

Use lists for fixed groups of customer profiles. Use segments when the audience should update automatically based on customer data or behavior.

Keep reading: [Understanding Lists and Segments]({% link _audience/lists-and-segments.md %}).

## Measure the result

After delivery, review clicks, conversions, customer activity, attributed revenue, and any replies that need follow-up in the inbox.
